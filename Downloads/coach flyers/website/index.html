FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

How much does a local move cost? +
The cost of a local move depends on the size of your home, the distance, and the services you need. A typical 2-bedroom local move on Long Island ranges from $800–$1,500. We provide free in-home estimates with a written, binding price — no surprises.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes. Coach Movers is fully licensed by the NY DOT, bonded, and insured. We carry full liability coverage and include our Full Value Protection Plan on every move at no additional cost.
Do you provide packing materials? +
Yes! We deliver free boxes, tape, bubble wrap, and wardrobe boxes right to your door before moving day. Our full packing service is also available — our trained team will professionally pack your entire home.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve all of Long Island (Nassau & Suffolk counties), New York City, the tri-state area, and long-distance moves to all 50 states. Popular service areas include Farmingdale, Massapequa, Garden City, Huntington, Syosset, and more.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance for local moves and 4–6 weeks for long-distance moves. However, we do accommodate last-minute moves when available — call us and we'll do our best.
What's included in the price? +
Every Coach Movers move includes over $1,200 in value: furniture wrapping and protection, floor and wall protection at both locations, furniture disassembly/reassembly, free box delivery, a dedicated move coordinator, and our Full Value Protection Plan.
Do you move specialty items like pianos? +
Yes. We handle pianos, antiques, artwork, wine collections, safes, and other high-value or oversized items. Our team uses specialized equipment and techniques for each item type.
What if something gets damaged? +
With our included Full Value Protection Plan, damaged items are covered at full replacement value — not the industry minimum of $0.60 per pound. If a $1,400 TV is damaged, we pay $1,400. Period.
