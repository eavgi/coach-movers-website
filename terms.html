2. Services Description

Coach Movers provides a comprehensive range of moving and related services, including:

  • Local moving services throughout Long Island, NYC, Connecticut, and New Jersey

  • Professional packing and unpacking services

3. Quotes and Estimates

All initial quotes and estimates provided by Coach Movers are non-binding preliminary estimates based on the information you provide. These estimates are designed to give you a general idea of potential costs but are subject to the following conditions:

  • Final pricing is based on the actual weight of your belongings, measured at time of pickup
  • Additional charges may apply for items requiring special handling or equipment
  • Travel time, distance, and route conditions may affect final pricing
  • Access restrictions, parking limitations, or building elevator unavailability may increase costs
  • On-site estimates are typically accurate within 10-15% of final invoice amounts
  • Video or phone estimates may have higher variance due to difficulty assessing all items

A formal binding quote will be provided in writing after an on-site inspection or detailed assessment of your move, if requested.

4. Booking and Cancellation Policy

Booking Confirmation: Moving dates are reserved upon receipt of a signed estimate and booking deposit. A confirmation email will be sent within 24 hours of booking.

Cancellation by Customer: The following cancellation policy applies:

  • Cancellations made 30 or more days prior to scheduled move date: Full refund of deposit
  • Cancellations made 15-29 days prior to scheduled move date: 50% refund of deposit
  • Cancellations made less than 15 days prior to scheduled move date: No refund of deposit
  • Cancellations made 72 hours or less prior to move date: Full moving charge applies
  • No-show (customer unavailable on scheduled date): Full moving charge applies

Rescheduling: Moves may be rescheduled without penalty if requested with at least 30 days' notice. Requests with less notice are subject to availability and may incur fees.

Cancellation by Coach Movers: Coach Movers reserves the right to cancel or reschedule services due to weather emergencies, equipment failure, illness of assigned crew, or other circumstances beyond our control. In such cases, we will provide prompt notification and offer alternative dates or a full refund of all payments made.

5. Liability and Insurance Coverage

Coach Movers maintains comprehensive liability and cargo insurance to protect your belongings during transport and storage. Please understand the following regarding coverage:

Coverage Type Details
Standard Valuation Included with all moves. Covers up to $0.60 per pound per item in case of loss or damage
Full Value Protection Optional upgrade. Covers declared value of items. Additional charge applies (typically 0.5-1% of move value)
High-Value Items Items valued over $5,000 require separate declaration and may require additional coverage
Storage Coverage Items stored in our facilities are covered under our warehouse insurance during storage period

Important Limitations: Insurance does not cover loss, damage, or disappearance of certain items including money, jewelry, artwork, collectibles, or items packed by the customer. For high-value items, please discuss coverage options with our moving consultant.

Damage Claims: Any claims for damage must be reported in writing within 10 days of delivery. Claims submitted after 30 days may not be eligible for compensation. Photographs and detailed descriptions of damaged items are required.

6. Customer Responsibilities

To ensure a smooth and safe moving experience, customers agree to:

  • Provide accurate and complete inventory of items to be moved
  • Disclose any fragile, valuable, or specialty items that require special handling
  • Ensure clear access to pickup and delivery locations (removing obstacles, arranging parking, scheduling building access)
  • Arrange for building manager approval if required by residential or commercial buildings
  • Provide elevator reservations if building access requires them
  • Ensure adequate space at delivery location for placement of furniture and boxes
  • Be present or have authorized representative present during pickup and delivery
  • Provide accurate floor plans and building dimensions if available
  • Arrange pet care or secure pets during moving operations for safety

7. Prohibited Items

For safety, legal, and insurance reasons, the following items cannot be transported by Coach Movers:

  • Hazardous materials (flammable liquids, explosives, compressed gases, chemicals)
  • Perishable food items
  • Medications and controlled substances
  • Firearms and ammunition
  • Propane grills and propane tanks (empty grills may be transported with customer approval)
  • Fertilizers and pesticides
  • Paint, varnish, and solvents
  • Batteries (loose or in equipment)
  • Live plants (generally; discuss specific requests with our team)
  • Items believed to be stolen or claimed by third parties
  • Any items that are illegal to transport under federal or state law

Customers are fully responsible for any prohibited items packed in boxes or containers. Coach Movers is not liable for any penalties, fines, or charges resulting from the transportation of prohibited items.

8. Payment Terms

Booking Deposit: A deposit of 10-25% of the estimated moving cost is required to confirm your moving date. This deposit is applied to your final invoice.

Final Payment: The balance of the moving invoice is due upon completion of services. We accept cash, check, credit card, and bank transfer. For moves exceeding $10,000, a 50% deposit may be required.

Payment for Additional Services: Any additional services provided beyond the original estimate (extra hours, additional boxes, specialty handling) are billed at standard rates upon completion.

Late Payments: Invoices are due within 30 days of service completion. Accounts unpaid after 60 days may be subject to late fees of 1.5% monthly interest or be referred to collection.

Price Guarantee: If you receive a written quote that is binding, the final price will not exceed that quote amount (excluding additional services not included in the original quote).
