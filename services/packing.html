Frequently Asked Questions

How long does packing a whole house take?

A typical 3-bedroom home takes our crew 4–6 hours to fully pack. Larger homes may take a full day. We can also pack the day before your move.

Do you provide the packing materials?

Yes! All packing supplies are included free of charge — boxes, tape, bubble wrap, wardrobe boxes, dish packs, and specialty crates.

Can I pack some things myself?

Absolutely. Many clients pack personal items and let us handle the kitchen, fragiles, and artwork. We're flexible.

How do you handle fragile items?

Fragile items are individually wrapped in packing paper and bubble wrap, then placed in double-walled boxes with cushioning. Artwork and mirrors get custom crating.

Do you offer unpacking services?

Yes. We offer full unpacking at your new home — placing items where you want them, breaking down boxes, and hauling away packing materials.
