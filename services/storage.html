Frequently Asked Questions

What can I store?

Furniture, appliances, electronics, clothing, documents, artwork, pianos — virtually anything except hazardous materials and perishables.

Is the storage facility climate controlled?

Yes. Our facility maintains consistent temperature and humidity levels year-round to protect your belongings from heat, cold, and moisture damage.

Can I access my items while they're in storage?

Yes, with advance notice. Contact your coordinator and we'll arrange access during business hours.

How much does storage cost?

Storage pricing depends on volume and duration. We offer competitive monthly rates starting from $150/month. Contact us for an exact quote.

Do you insure items in storage?

Yes. All items in our storage are covered by our protection plan. Additional coverage options are available for high-value items.
