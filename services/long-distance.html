Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a long-distance move cost?

Long-distance moves are priced based on weight, distance, and services. A typical 2-bedroom move from NY to FL ranges from $3,000–$6,000. Contact us for an exact quote.

How long does a long-distance move take?

Transit time depends on distance. East Coast moves typically take 3–7 days. Cross-country moves take 7–14 days. Your coordinator will provide an exact timeline.

Is my stuff on a shared truck?

No. Coach Movers uses dedicated trucks for long-distance moves. Your belongings are the only ones on the truck — no shared loads.

What about storage during a long-distance move?

If your new home isn't ready yet, we offer climate-controlled storage at our secure facility for any duration.

Do you handle specialty items for long-distance moves?

Absolutely. Pianos, antiques, artwork, wine collections — we custom-crate high-value items for maximum protection during transit.
