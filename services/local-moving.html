Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a local move on Long Island cost?

Local move costs depend on home size, distance, and services needed. A typical 2-bedroom apartment on Long Island ranges from $600–$1,200. Get an exact quote by contacting us.

Do you offer same-day moving?

Yes! We offer same-day and next-day local moving services subject to crew availability. Call us for urgent move requests.

What's included in a local move?

Every local move includes furniture wrapping, floor protection, mattress covers, basic disassembly/reassembly, and a dedicated team lead.

How long does a local move take?

Most studio/1-bedroom moves take 2–4 hours. Larger homes (3–5 bedrooms) typically take 4–8 hours depending on complexity.

Do I need to be home during the move?

We recommend being present for the walkthrough at the start and end, but you don't need to be there the entire time.
